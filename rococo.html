Rococo Postage Stamp
Inspired by the Rococo art movement, these postage stamps feature cherubs, a symbol seen commonly in art from this movement. The colours used are soft pastels, reflecting the light and whimsical nature of this art movement, while the ornamental and asymmetrical border repesents the ornamnetal style of Rococo art and architecture. The background of the stamps are covered in light, barely visible bushstrokes, similar to the painting style of this movement. Inspired by the love and whimsy of this movement, the poster says, "Send your letters with love," referring to the many artworks about lovers during this period.
