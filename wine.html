Romanticism Wine Label
Taking inspiration from the Romantic art movement, full of self-reflection and introspection, this wine label and poster were created. Heavily influenced by the works of William Blake, the label features a tree split in two parts - the tree of life, and the tree of death. The main focus lies on the two devilish figures mirroring each other, demons and the supernatural being a common otif in the Romantic period. The poster says, "Lose your mind, find your soul," alluding to the state of being wine-drunk and this state leading to self-discovery, which is one of the most common themes in Romantic art and literature.
